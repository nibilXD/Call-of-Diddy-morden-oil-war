Call of Diddy: Modern Oil War

Game Description

“Slide into the smoothest battlefield ever.”

In a world torn apart by dryness and chaos, only one man dares to fight with hydration. Command the elite Diddy Ops Unit in the most slippery, over-the-top FPS parody of the decade. Armed with baby lotion rifles, tactical aloe strikes, and coconut-scented grenades, you’ll moisturize your way through oil-rich warzones in search of hydration... and domination. Whether it’s Multiplayer Grease Pit, Campaign Mode: Operation Hydration, or Lotion Royale, victory smells like lavender. Lather up. Lock on. Oil out. The war for moisture has begun.
